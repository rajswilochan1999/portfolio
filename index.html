Recently i have worked on a machine learning project named -"Prediction and Analysis of Degree of Suicidal Ideation in Online Content" under supervision of Dr. Akshay Deepak as a part of project in Machine learning course and enjoyed developing various models and implementing various NLP techniques, our project was recognized in top 5% . In this project , Machine learning to infer suicide risk and urgency is applied to a dataset of Reddit users in which the risk and urgency labels were derived from crowdsource consensus. For this project we had used Reddit’s API to collect posts from two subreddits: “r/depression” and “r/Suicidewatch”. After selecting the relevant features out of many collected from the above source, we performed data cleaning, preprocessing and Exploratory Data Analysis. Models which gave best accuracy for our dataset were : 1. TFIDFVectorizer + Logistic Regression, 2. TFIDFVectorizer + Multinomial Naive Bayes.
